DONOR RULES

  • Age Limit: Donors must be above 18 years of age for self-pledging.
  • Medical Honesty: Ensure medical history is accurate to prevent transplant failure.
  • Consent: Inform your family about your pledge; they are the ultimate decision-makers.
  • Legality: Organ donation is a gift of life; buying/selling organs is a criminal offense.
