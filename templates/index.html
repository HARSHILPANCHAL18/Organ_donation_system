About OrganFlow

OrganFlow is a smart organ donation and management platform designed to connect donors, hospitals, and patients through a secure digital system. Our goal is to increase awareness about organ donation and make the registration and matching process simple, transparent, and accessible. One donor can save multiple lives — and OrganFlow helps make that possible.

Our Mission

Promote organ donation and save lives using technology.

Our Vision

Build a transparent and trusted organ donation network.

Our Impact

Helping donors and patients connect faster and securely.
